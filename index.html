The crossroads are not an ending. They are where the self returns.

Hosted by Ashley

A place for the parts of us learning to come home.

Salt & Sovereignty grew out of my own journey through trauma, therapy, witchcraft, and devotion to Hekate. I do not claim to have all the answers. I share what I am learning as I walk this path, including the doubts, the breakthroughs, the messy middle, and the quiet moments of magic that remind me I am not alone.

This space is rooted in witchcraft, shadow work, embodiment, spiritual awakening, and the long, imperfect work of reclaiming your power. My hope is that what I share feels less like instruction from above and more like a lantern set down beside you at the crossroads.

Here, we make room for

  • Hekatean devotion and crossroads spirituality
  • Trauma-informed shadow work
  • Embodiment, ritual, and nervous system care
  • Sacred storytelling and self-reclamation
